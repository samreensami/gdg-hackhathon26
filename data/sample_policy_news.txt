GOVERNMENT ANNOUNCES PETROLEUM LEVY HIKE
Date: May 14, 2026

ISLAMABAD — In a move aimed at meeting international revenue targets, the government today announced an immediate 15% increase in the petroleum development levy. 

This policy change will have immediate and far-reaching impacts on the transport and logistics industry across Pakistan. Early analysis from the Pakistan Freight Association suggests that companies handling last-mile delivery will be hit the hardest. 

Industry experts predict that standard delivery fees will need to be revised upward, and companies without agile routing capabilities will suffer severe margin compression. The new rates are effective starting from the first of next month, leaving logistics providers little time to adapt their business models.
